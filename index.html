01 / FAILURE REGISTER

Where the commercial motion actually breaks.

The formal question is what the meeting says. The operator translation is what the field hears.

  • Decision storyDoes the story create a decision?
  • Stakeholder pathwayCan decisions and handoffs survive without rescue?
  • Field transferCan the motion become repeatable use?

Decision story

Commercial story Does the pitch create a decision? The deck has 47 slides and no decision.

Pressure-test: Which decision should this meeting create? What can disappear without weakening it?

Stakeholder pathway

Leadership Is ownership unambiguous? Everyone owns it, so no one owns it.

Pressure-test: Who owns the next move, by when, and who only needs visibility?

Team build Are roles connected by an operating system? You hired résumés and hoped they would form a company.

Pressure-test: Which handoff currently depends on goodwill, memory, or rescue?

Field transfer

Sales motion Can the process work without one heroic person? The process is scalable, provided the founder attends every call.

Pressure-test: What breaks when the heroic person stays home?

Launch readiness Does the work support the calendar? The calendar says launch. The work was not consulted.

Pressure-test: Which decision, evidence handoff, or field capability must be real before the date means anything?

When these recurring signals persist across functions, the diagnosis is direct.

The commercial pathway is broken.

A funnel is not a buying environment.

Map who uses, champions, funds, approves, implements, and learns. Then mark the handoff where momentum dies.

ENGAGEMENT / 01

Pathway Autopsy

Use when
Deals stall at different handoffs and every function offers a different diagnosis.
Hand-back
An annotated stakeholder pathway, named failure points, priority decisions, owners, and next moves.
Working format
A cross-functional review of one live commercial pathway: stakeholders, decisions, evidence, ownership, and handoffs.
Who joins
The commercial owner, field lead, the person responsible for clinical adoption, and owners of the handoffs under review.

ENGAGEMENT / 02

Pitch Decontamination

Use when
The deck explains the product but still does not create a decision.
Hand-back
A decision narrative, evidence sequence, unresolved questions, and a claims-review handoff for the appropriate internal owners.
Working format
A red-team pass against actual buyer roles, responsible evidence, decision sequence, and internal claims-review boundaries.
Who joins
The pitch owner, commercial lead, the person representing clinical reality, and appropriate internal evidence or claims owners.

ENGAGEMENT / 03

Field Transfer

Use when
The commercial motion depends on one founder, veteran, or heroic field operator.
Hand-back
A field operating map, ownership matrix, handoff checklist, and route for feeding wins and losses back into the system.
Working format
A working review of repeatable moves, role boundaries, handoffs, and field-learning loops.
Who joins
The commercial leader, field representatives, operations or enablement owners, and relevant clinical-adoption partners.

Engineering discipline
