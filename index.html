01 / BUYING PATHWAY UNDER LOAD

The funnel is not the buying environment.

These roles are often different people. A credible pathway—and the deck explaining it—must understand the difference.

  1. 01 / USER

    User

    Needs: A workable change in the real use environment, with friction made visible.

  2. 02 / CHAMPION

    Clinical champion

    Needs: Defensible value, responsible proof, and language that earns stakeholder trust.

  3. 03 / BUYER

    Economic buyer

    Needs: A clear decision, a credible tradeoff, and a reason to allocate resources.

  4. 04 / APPROVER

    Approver or committee

    Needs: Claims discipline, risk clarity, and evidence fit for the decision at hand.

  5. 05 / IMPLEMENTATION

    Procurement and implementation

    Needs: Terms, owners, handoffs, and an executable path from approval to use.

  6. 06 / FIELD LEARNING

    Field learning and repeatability

    Needs: A feedback loop that turns individual wins and losses into a motion the team can repeat.

LENS / ENGINEERING

Engineering lens

04 / OPERATING PROCEDURE

Move from assumption to accountable action.

Five-step operating procedure
Input Action Output
Intake Isolate assumptions. The real decision is defined.
Dual pass Compare clinical and engineering/commercial failure signals. Contradictions and shared constraints are visible.
Red team Pressure-test story, sequence, ownership, and handoffs. Failure points are named in operating language.
Rebuild Identify the smallest executable corrections. A corrected pathway can be put to work.
Hand back Establish accountable owners. Explicit next moves, owners, and decision points.

05 / WAYS TO USE THE DUO

Bring a live commercial problem.

Pathway Autopsy

Use when: A deal, launch, or sales motion keeps stalling without a shared explanation.

Leave with: A mapped buying pathway, named failure points, and explicit next decisions.

Pitch Decontamination

Use when: The story explains the product but does not help a stakeholder decide.

Leave with: A disciplined decision narrative, evidence sequence, and language the field can responsibly use.

Field Transfer

Use when: Commercial knowledge lives with a few heroic people or disappears at every handoff.

Leave with: Clear roles, operating handoffs, feedback loops, and accountable next moves.

06 / CONTROLLED BURN

Remove the ceremonial language. Keep the operating truth.

  • A funnel is a picture. Buying is behavior.
  • Alignment without action is synchronized nodding.
  • Claims discipline is commercial discipline.
  • If nobody owns it, it is not a next step.
  • If the pathway only works in PowerPoint, burn the map.
