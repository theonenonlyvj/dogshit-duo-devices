02 / WAYS TO USE THE DUO

Bring the problem that keeps surviving the meeting.

Pathway Autopsy

Use when: Deals stall at different points and every team has a different diagnosis.

Leave with: The stakeholder path, named failure points, next decisions, and owners.

Pitch Decontamination

Use when: The deck explains the product and still does not create a decision.

Leave with: A disciplined decision narrative, evidence sequence, and claims-review handoff for the appropriate internal owners.

Field Transfer

Use when: The motion works only when one founder or veteran is present.

Leave with: Repeatable roles, handoffs, feedback loops, and accountable next moves.

03 / BUYING ROLES & HANDOFFS

A funnel is not a buying environment.

Map who uses, champions, funds, approves, implements, and learns. Then mark the handoff where momentum dies.

The CRM has one field for “decision-maker.” Reality brought a committee.

  1. 01 / USER

    User

    Must decide: Whether the change works in the real use environment.

    Breaks when: Workflow burden and adoption friction stay invisible.

  2. 02 / CHAMPION

    Clinical champion

    Must decide: Whether the value is defensible enough to carry forward.

    Breaks when: The story outruns responsible evidence and stakeholder trust.

  3. 03 / BUYER

    Economic buyer

    Must decide: Whether the tradeoff deserves resources now.

    Breaks when: The ask, owner, or economic consequence remains vague.

  4. 04 / APPROVER

    Approver or committee

    Must decide: Whether evidence, risk, and claims fit the decision.

    Breaks when: Questions surface after the meeting that the pitch never prepared for.

  5. 05 / IMPLEMENTATION

    Procurement and implementation

    Must decide: Whether evidence fit, resource impact, terms, and ownership support use.

    Breaks when: Approval arrives without workable handoffs or an implementation path.

  6. 06 / FIELD LEARNING

    Field learning and repeatability

    Must decide: Which win and loss signals should change the motion.

    Breaks when: Learning stays with individuals instead of changing the system.

04 / OPERATING METHOD

Two passes. One accountable hand-back.

PASS / CLINICAL REALITY

Clinical reality

Use environment, behavior change, adoption friction, stakeholder trust, and whether the story survives contact with care delivery.

PASS / COMMERCIAL SYSTEM

Engineering and commercial system

Constraints, decision mechanics, ownership, handoffs, field feedback, and whether the motion can repeat without a hero.

HAND-BACK / FIVE MOVES

  1. Pin the real decision.
  2. Run the clinical-reality pass.
  3. Run the engineering and commercial-system pass.
  4. Red-team the story, evidence, ownership, and handoffs.
  5. Assign the smallest next moves, owners, and dates.

05 / CONTROLLED BURN

Remove the ceremonial language. Keep the operating truth.

  • A funnel is a picture. Buying is behavior.
  • Alignment without an owner is synchronized nodding.
  • If it only works in PowerPoint, it does not work.

06 / USEFUL NEXT ACTION

  3. What proof can the field responsibly use?
  4. Where does momentum actually die?
